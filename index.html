About Modura Analytics Kazi Pro

Our Mission

Modura Analytics Kazi Pro is a free, browser-based career tool designed to help job seekers create professional, ATS-friendly resumes, cover letters, and job application emails. Built by Modura Analytics, a data and research consultancy, Kazi Pro combines expertise in data-driven decision making with practical career development tools.

Why We Built This

Many job seekers struggle with creating resumes that pass through Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS). These automated systems scan resumes for keywords, formatting, and structure before a human ever sees them. Poorly formatted resumes are often rejected before reaching a recruiter.

Kazi Pro solves this problem by generating clean, simple, ATS-friendly documents using rule-based logic. No artificial intelligence, no data collection, no hidden costs.

How It Works

1. Fill in your personal details, skills, experience, and target job information.
2. The builder generates a clean, ATS-friendly resume, cover letter, or email.
3. Copy the text, download as PDF, or download as Word.
4. Use the ATS Checklist to verify your resume score before submitting.

Privacy First

All data is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. No information is sent to any server. Your personal data never leaves your device. When you close the browser, your data is deleted (unless stored in localStorage for your convenience).
